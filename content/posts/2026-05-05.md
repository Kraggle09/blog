I love this game so much. I started playing in October on a Roblox clone, then got the actual game (which is free, not sure why I didn't do it earlier) in November, then finally got an account in December. By that point, I was decent at the game, so I got to rank 1.2 million in the first weekend. I think it took a couple weeks before I got to top million, and then I'd go up 100k ranks every couples weeks or so. I finally started plateauing in March, after a bunch of farming. I was at rank 600k or so by that point. Recently though, in like, April, I started getting better again. Setting new high scores, finding it easier to do higher difficulty songs, etc.

And that brings me to last weekend, where I set my first >100pp play. My previous best play was 98pp, but I got (on the same beatmap) 114PP!! Which is a crazy difference. I thought "Okay well, it'll be a while before I can get another score like that" Nope! I got a 104pp play right after. Then a few plays later, I got a 100pp play. I got 3 >100pp plays in one day. Very happy about that.
